Mila Victoria Fossey

is an electronic musician, composer, producer, and sound designer based in Brussels, Belgium.

Mila Fossey is a 20 year-old sonic artist from France and Romania. She is an artist, musician, songwriter, sound designer, producer, and performer. Having grown up in China, Canada, France, and Belgium, she has been musically influenced by the environments surrounding her and is developing her own sound through her experimental vocal and instrumental style. With a strong passion for protest art, music is the translation of fusion between her ideas and inspirations into prototypes. She has been showcasing her original work for over 12 years through different creative communities and has been mainly creating electronic modular and vocal pieces in the past year. With experience as a songwriter in folk, pop, electronic, and experimental music, and playing over 4 instruments, she equally creates sound art pieces for performance and contemporary art installations. Music is the translation of her soul.

MILA AND GRACE

are a harp and vocal duo who delve into experimental, ambient, and classical music influences. They are inspired by electronic, classical, and folk music.

The group consists of Mila (vocalist), and Grace (harpist), who come together to write a variety of original pieces. Together, they cultivate a vast, lush soundscape.
